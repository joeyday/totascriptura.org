This category contains topics and subcategories pertaining to **[soteriology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soteriology)**, or the study of salvation.

From Wikipedia:

> **Soteriology** is the theological doctrine of salvation as effected by Jesus Christ. It is derived from the Greek sōtērion “salvation” \[from sōtēr “savior, preserver”) + logos (word)\].[^1]
