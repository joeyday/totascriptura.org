This category contains topics and subcategories pertaining to **[eschatology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eschatology)**, or the study of “last things,” e.g., death, the afterlife, or the end of the world.

From Wikipedia:

> **Eschatology** (from the Greek ἔσχατος, Eschatos meaning “last” and -logy meaning “the study of”) is a part of theology and philosophy concerned with what is believed to be the final events in the history of the world, or the ultimate destiny of humanity, commonly referred to as the end of the world.[^1]
