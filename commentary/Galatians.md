**Gal 6:4** **His reason to boast will be in himself alone and not in his neighbor**. Since the major theme of the epistle is justification by faith, and considering the immediate context (Gal 6:3, 14), Paul cannot be saying people can boast before God about their good works. It’s possible Paul holds out a real sense in which we can be proud of our own work (and that only as Christ works through us; cf. Ro 15:17–18), but even so the emphasis seems to be more on examining our own work without comparing ourselves to others.
