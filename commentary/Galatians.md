Gal 6:4 **His reason to boast will be in himself alone**. Paul cannot be saying people can boast before God about their good works. In Gal 6:14 he clarifies he has nothing to boast in but the cross. In Ro 15:17–18 he talks about being proud of his own work because Christ is the one working through him. Here in Galatians the emphasis seems to be on avoiding comparing ourselves to others and simply examining our own work by itself.
