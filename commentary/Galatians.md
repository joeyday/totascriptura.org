**Gal 6:4** **His reason to boast will be in himself alone and not in his neighbor**. Paul cannot be saying people can boast before God about their good works. The major theme of the epistle to the Galatians is justification by faith, not by works. In Gal 6:3 he says anyone who thinks he’s something is deceiving himself and in Gal 6:14 he clarifies he has nothing to boast in but the cross. If there’s any sense in which we can be proud of our own work it’s only as Christ works through us (Ro 15:17–18). Here the emphasis seems to be on examining our own work without comparing ourselves to others.
