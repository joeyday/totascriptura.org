## Outline

1. Greeting (Ro 1:1–15)
2. Thesis (Ro 1:16–17)
3. Total depravity (Ro 1:18–3:20)
4. Justification (Ro 3:21–5:21)
5. Sanctification (Ro 6:1–8:17)
6. Glorification (Ro 8:18–8:39)
7. Unconditional election (Ro 9–11)
8. Practical application and exhortation (Ro 12:1–15:13)
9. Farewell and benediction (Ro 15:14–16:27)

## Romans 1

**Ro 1:8** **I thank my God**. Paul thanks God for the faith of the church in Rome. This only makes sense if faith is a gift from God in the first place (see [[topic/Effectual grace|Effectual grace]]).
