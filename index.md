Hi, I’m Joey. This project is my small contribution to Christian systematic theology. I’m endeavoring to bring “the total weight of the teaching of Scripture”[^grudem] to bear on many important topics here. For more on how and why the site came to be, read about this project.

You’re welcome to browse my favorite topics or all topics alphabetically or categorically, or use the search box if you’re looking for something specific. You can even visit a random topic if you’re feeling lucky.

If anything here either helps you or is clearly incorrect or unhelpful, you’re welcome to send feedback via [Mastodon](https://mastodon.social/@joeyday) or [Facebook](http://facebook.com/joeynday).

[^grudem]: Grudem, Wayne (2009). _Systematic Theology: An Introduction to Biblical Doctrine_ (p. 28). Zondervan.
