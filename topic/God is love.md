## God’s “steadfast love”

The ESV translates the Hebrew חֶ֫סֶד (*hesed*) consistently as “steadfast love”, though some other translations render it simply “mercy”. Given that these passages may not be about love *per se*, I’ve split them into their own section here.

### God’s “steadfast love” for Israel, the fathers

### God’s “steadfast love” for David, the king of Israel

### God’s “steadfast love” for those who love him, serve him, keep his commandments, etc.
