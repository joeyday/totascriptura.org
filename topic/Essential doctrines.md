[^necessary]: A **necessary doctrine** <small>(e.g. *“if you don’t believe this you cannot be saved”*)</small> must be believed for salvation but may not be enough to save by itself. A **sufficient doctrine** <small>(e.g. *“if you believe this you will be saved”*)</small> is enough to save by itself, but may go beyond what is strictly necessary for salvation. Considered logically, a correct understanding of any of the sufficient doctrines must in some way contain or presuppose all the necessary ones.
