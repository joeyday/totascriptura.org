The **Great Law** is a set of complementary doctrinal ideas composed and described by a friend of mine. I do not necessarily agree with these ideas, but am documenting them here so I can study and ponder them. You should use discernment when reading this page.

### Law of desire

Ps 37:4; 145:15–16; Pr 10:24 ~— *see also Ps 20:4; 21:2*~

### Law of faith

Mt 17:20 ~(cf. Mk 9:23; Lk 17:6)~; Mt 21:21–22 ~(cf. Mk 11:22–24)~; Heb 11:1

### Ask

Mt 7:7–11 ~(cf. Lk 11:9–13)~; Mt 21:22 ~(cf. Mk 11:24)~; Lk 11:5–8; 18:1–8

### Law of harvest

Job 4:8; Pr 12:14; 22:8; Hos 8:7; 10:12–13; 2Co 9:6; Gal 6:7 ~— *see also Mt 5:45; Ac 14:16–17*~

### Law of creation

Hebrews 11:3

### Law of attraction

Pr 11:23–27; Mt 6:14–15; 7:1–2 ~(cf. Mk 4:24; Lk 6:38)~

### The power of thoughts

Pr 23:7 KJV; Lk 2:35

### How God punishes the wicked

Jdg 1:6–7; Ps 5:10; 7:14–16; 9:16; Pr 1:31; 14:14; 18:7; Isa 3:11; Jer 6:19; Eze 35:6, 11; Hab 3:14

### Examples of extreme desire or faith

Mt 9:20–22 ~(cf. Mk 5:25–29; Lk 8:42–48)~; Mt 14:25–31; Lk 7:2–10

### What we should think about

Jos 1:8; Ps 1:1–2; 19:7–10; 73:25–26; Pr 3:6; 1Co 12:31; 14:1, 39; Php 4:8

### Uncategorized

Mt 6:1–6, 16–21; 2Co 8:10–12; 1Pe 3:9
