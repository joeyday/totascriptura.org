## Bible vs. Book of Mormon

Interesting difference between the epistemology of the Bible vs. the epistemology of the Book of Mormon.

* The Bible says whatever is from God will confess that Jesus has come in the flesh (1Jn 4:1–6).
* The Book of Mormon says that whatever is from God will entice you to do good works (Moro 7:13).
