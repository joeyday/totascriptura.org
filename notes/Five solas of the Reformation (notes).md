## What the five solas were reacting against

- Sola Scriptura (“Scripture alone”) vs. Scripture + Tradition and Magisterium
- Sola fide (“faith alone”) vs. faith + works
- Sola gratia (“grace alone”) vs. grace + merit
- Solus Christus (“Christ alone”) vs. Christ + the Pope and priestly order
- Soli Deo gloria (“glory to God alone”) vs. glory to God + veneration of Mary, saints, and angels
