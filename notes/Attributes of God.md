## More passages on wisdom
Steven Lawson defines God’s wisdom as his seeking the highest ends by the highest means. To demonstrate God’s wisdom in these terms, he cites several verses about creation to show that God seeks the highest end, his own glory: Ps 19:1; Ro 1:20. He also cites several portions of Job to show God’s wisdom in his care over and ordering of all things: end of Job; Ge 50:20; Ac 17:26–27; Ro 8:28. Lastly he talks of God’s wisdom in salvation: 1Co 1:27.
