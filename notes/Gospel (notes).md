## Quote from Dr. Frank James

Dr. Frank James says in his “Greek Apologists and Early Theologians” lecture (track 8) in the [RTS “History of Christianity I” course on iTunes U](http://itunes.apple.com/WebObjects/MZStore.woa/wa/viewPodcast?id=378878750) that you have to believe very little to be saved. I think there's a bit of hypocrisy going on here. We say you have to believe very little to be saved, but then when somebody says they're Mormon all of a sudden we get jumpy and conclude without very much objectivity that they just can't be saved. Why is this? We need to be more reasoned and objective if we are to make a case against false doctrine on all sides, not just Mormonism. —**Joey** 21:03, 28 November 2010 (UTC)
