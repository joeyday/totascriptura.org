## Disclaimer

This is a controversial subject. God is omnibenevolent, yet omniscient and omnipotent. Evil exists in the world, and, as I see it, the only way that’s possible is if God somehow ordained evil to be, yet without sinning himself.

These passages (on the [[topic/Deception|topic]] page) seem to suggest that God has lied, deceived, and kept secret things hidden from men’s understanding. To be faithful to all of God’s word, we must affirm that somehow he did these things and yet without sin. —**Joey** 15:45, 24 March 2011 (UTC)
